# Quiz Exercise

## Overview

Quiz exercises allow you to test your knowledge through different types of questions including multiple-choice, drag-and-drop, and short-answer questions. Quizzes can be timed and may have specific start times depending on how your instructor configured them.

## Participating in a Quiz

If a quiz exercise is available on the Artemis home page as a current exercise, you will be able to see it in the course overview or inside the course where it belongs.

<Image src={currentQuizExercise} alt="Current Quiz Exercise" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Current Quiz Exercise Overview" />

To start the quiz, press the <img src={openQuizButton} alt="Open Quiz" style={{display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'middle', height: '2rem'}} /> button.

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.info}>
  <p>If the quiz is set to start at a specific time and you open it before, you will see a message asking you to wait until the quiz starts and displaying the remaining time.</p>
</Callout>

<Image src={pleaseWaitMessage} alt="Please Wait Message" size={ImageSize.medium} caption="Waiting for Quiz to Start" />

## Solving the Quiz

When the quiz starts, you can see the questions and solve them.

<Image src={oneChoiceQuestion} alt="One Choice Question" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Quiz Question View" />

The quiz page is composed of:

- **Options**:
  - Options with circles mean one choice could be correct
  - Options with squares mean multiple options could be correct
- Last time saved: The quiz will save all changes automatically
- <img src={submitButton} alt="Submit" style={{display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'middle', height: '2rem'}} /> button: To allow you to submit the quiz before the time ends

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.warning}>
  <p>The quiz automatically saves your changes as you make them. However, when the quiz time's up, the answers will be submitted automatically.</p>
</Callout>

### Drag-and-Drop Questions

In the case of Drag and Drop questions, the items to be dragged and dropped in the spots will be available on the right side.

<Image src={dragAndDropView} alt="Drag and Drop View" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Drag-and-Drop Question View" />

To submit and finish the quiz, press the <img src={submitButton} alt="Submit" style={{display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'middle', height: '2rem'}} /> button. However, when the quiz time's up, the answers will be submitted automatically.

## Viewing Results

The assessment is automatic and you can see the result of the overall quiz and of specific questions. In the case of multiple-choice questions, the solution will be displayed.

<Image src={finalQuiz} alt="Final Quiz" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Quiz Results Overview" />

In the case of Drag and Drop questions, the solution is shown by clicking the <img src={showSampleSolutionButton} alt="Show Sample Solution" style={{display: 'inline', verticalAlign: 'middle', height: '2rem'}} /> button.

<Image src={solutionDragAndDrop} alt="Solution Drag and Drop" size={ImageSize.large} caption="Drag-and-Drop Solution View" />

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.info}>
  <p>After completing a quiz, you can review your answers and compare them with the correct solutions to learn from your mistakes.</p>
</Callout>
