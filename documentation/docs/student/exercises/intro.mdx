# Exercises

## Overview

Artemis offers different exercise types to help you practice and demonstrate your knowledge. Each exercise type has its own characteristics and ways of submission.

### Exercise Types

Artemis supports the following exercise types:

- **Quiz Exercises**: Test your knowledge with multiple-choice, drag-and-drop, and short-answer questions
- **Modeling Exercises**: Create UML diagrams and other models using the integrated Apollon editor
- **Programming Exercises**: Write and submit code solutions with automated testing and feedback
- **Textual Exercises**: Write free-text answers to open-ended questions
- **File Upload Exercises**: Submit files such as documents, images, or other content

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.info}>
  <p>All exercises share common properties such as release dates, due dates, and assessment due dates. Learn more about exercise organization in the <a href="/student/general">General Information</a> section.</p>
</Callout>

### Common Exercise Features

All exercises share these common features:

- **Release Date**: The date when the exercise becomes visible to you
- **Due Date**: The deadline for submitting your solution
- **Assessment Due Date**: The date when you can view feedback from instructors
- **Points**: Points you can earn, which may count toward your course score or serve as bonus points

### Getting Started

Select an exercise type from the sidebar to learn more about how to participate and submit your solutions.

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.warning}>
  <p>Make sure to submit your solutions before the due date. Late submissions may not be accepted depending on your instructor's settings.</p>
</Callout>
