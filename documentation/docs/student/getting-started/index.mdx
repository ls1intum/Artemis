# Getting Started

Welcome to Artemis! This section helps you get set up and familiar with the platform.

## First Steps

1. **Log in** to your university's Artemis instance using your institutional credentials.
2. **Enroll in a course** by navigating to the course list and clicking the **Course Enrollment** button. Some courses may have prerequisites you need to meet before enrolling.
3. **Explore the course sidebar.** Once inside a course, use the left sidebar to navigate to exercises, lectures, exams, communication channels, and more. The available items depend on your course's configuration.
4. **Start working.** Open an exercise from the sidebar, read the problem statement, and submit your solution before the due date.

<Callout variant={CalloutVariant.info}>
You can collapse the course sidebar with **Ctrl+M** and quickly switch between courses using the course icon at the top of the sidebar.
</Callout>

## Key Concepts

- **Exercises** have a release date, a due date, and an assessment due date. Points are awarded based on your submissions and may count toward your final course score.
- **Feedback** is provided after the assessment due date, either automatically (e.g., for programming and quiz exercises) or manually by tutors.
- **Competencies** are learning objectives that tie together lectures and exercises. Your progress and mastery are tracked automatically.
- **Communication** channels let you ask questions, discuss with peers, and receive announcements directly within each course.
